Paris – Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is located in the heart of Paris, France. It is one of the most iconic landmarks in the world and offers amazing city views from the top.

✨ Best for: sightseeing and photography
⏰ Best time: evening for sunset views
📌 Tip: Visit at night to see the light show

Athens – Parthenon

The Parthenon is an ancient temple located on the Acropolis hill in Athens, Greece. It is one of the most important symbols of Ancient Greek civilization and architecture.

✨ Best for: history lovers and photography
⏰ Best time: early morning or sunset to avoid crowds and heat
📌 Tip: wear comfortable shoes—The walk up the acropolis involves stairs and uneven paths

New York City – Statue of Liberty

The Statue of Liberty is a colossal neoclassical sculpture located on Liberty Island in New York Harbor, United States. It was a gift from France and has become one of the most recognizable symbols of freedom and democracy in the world.

✨ Best for: history enthusiasts, cultural exploration, and iconic photography
⏰ Best time: early morning or late afternoon for fewer crowds and softer lighting
📌 Tip: Book ferry tickets in advance and take time to explore nearby Ellis Island for a deeper historical experience
